Who We Are

Naacas Sevens Sustainable Procurement Solutions is a webapp that evaluates procurement companies on the above mentioned practices and makes recommendations to organisation looking to procure through strategic sustainable means. It also eases the whole process of procurement by centralising the supply capabilities, financial capability and previous sustainable methods implemented by the procurement company. It also offers the ability of organisation to track the procurement process and recommend the procurement company.

Naacas Sevens Sustainable Procurement Solutions comes into play to solve the issue of companies trying to acquire sustainable procurement, by sourcing, vetting and connecting organisations that embrace and implement the following strategic sustainable practices:

  • Circular resources
  • Carbon positivity
  • Land use
  • Water use
  • Healthy Community
  • Consumer Wellbeing
  • Fair business
